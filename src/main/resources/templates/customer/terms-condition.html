General Terms:
  • Requests must be submitted at least 14 days prior to event date
  • TCU has until 5 days before the date of the appearance to accept or decline
  • Prices are based on a 1 hour event
  • Additional fees may apply if more than one hour
  • Appearances are only available in the DFW area
  • The SuperFrog suit will not be rented out to a third party
  • Due to university rules, TCU Spirit will not make appearances during study days, finals week, or major holidays including, but not limited to, New Years Eve and Day, MLK Day, 4th of July, Thanksgiving Day, Christmas Eve or Day.
Contact Terms:
  • Unless otherwise specified, the contact provided and their information will also serve as our Spirit member's point-of-contact on the date of the event
  • List the address and all other necessary information on the booking site
Event terms:
  • Visit Super Frog Schedulerto see our current schedule and book your apearance request
  • Submission of a request does not guarantee the event will be accepted. TCu Spirit has until 5 days prior the date of the event to accept or decline a request.
  • Even after an event is confirmed, it is still subjected to immediate cancellation due to a TCU Athletic Department function, weather, or an emergency. Immediate notice will be given in the event of cancellation.
Transportation Terms:
  • Must include mileage if location is more than 2 miles from TCU and include any parking fees/expenses
  • If more than 2 miles from TCU, a $0.75 per mile transportation fee will be included in the appearance total
  • 6+ Spirit Squad members will require transporation to be booked to and from Fort Worth and paid for by requestor
Payment Terms:
  • All appearances are paid. We do not offer volunteer appearances
  • Payment should be submitted a minimum of 24 hours prior to event or it will be cancelled.
  • You will be given a link for payment via credit card upon approval of your appearance through email.
